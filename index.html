The Experience

An immersive offline experience where LOEWE's craftsmanship meets On's performance technology. Set within a hemispherical, tech-driven space, movement becomes a shared language — connecting urban structure with natural motion.

Participants step into a controlled environment designed to blur boundaries between fashion, function, and physical experience.

The Journey

Visitors enter the space in pairs, wearing co-branded CLOUDTILT shoes equipped with foot-tracking technology. Each participant represents a different perspective — LOEWE (urban) and On (outdoor).

Through synchronized real-world movement, two separate environments gradually merge into one unified world, expressing the collection's core philosophy: from contrast to harmony, from city to nature.

Beyond the Space

Upon completing the experience, participants receive a limited-edition leather keepsake embossed with the game’s ripple motif. A personal experience archive — including recorded moments from the journey — is automatically saved to your account. The experience extends beyond the physical space, becoming a lasting connection between movement, memory, and design.
